Frequently Asked
How long does delivery take?
Usually 20-40 minutes within Vijayawada. Exact time depends on your distance from the store and order size.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel before the merchant starts preparing. After that, cancellation may not be possible.
How do refunds work?
Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method, or instantly to your ManaBazar wallet.
Do I need a prescription?
Schedule H medicines require a valid prescription. You can upload a photo of your prescription before checkout. OTC medicines don't need one.
