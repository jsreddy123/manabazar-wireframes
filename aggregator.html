About Us

ManaBazar

Local First. Digital Always.

ManaBazar is Vijayawada's own hyperlocal discovery and commerce platform. We connect customers with trusted local stores — pharmacies, restaurants, grocery shops, bakeries, meat shops, and more — all within your neighbourhood.

Our Mission

To digitise every local business in Andhra Pradesh and give them the tools to compete with national chains — starting with Vijayawada and Guntur. We believe local stores are the backbone of our communities, and they deserve modern technology at affordable prices.

How It Works

  • For Customers: Discover stores near you, browse products, place orders via WhatsApp or online, and get home delivery — all from shops you already know and trust.
  • For Merchants: Get a free online storefront, appear on our aggregator, accept WhatsApp orders, and manage your business with our optional software tools (YouPharma, YouRetail, YouResto).

Why Local?

When you order from a local store, the money stays in your community. The store owner is your neighbour. The delivery is faster because they are closer. And the products are fresh because they are sourced locally. We are building technology to strengthen these existing relationships, not replace them.

Our Products

  • ManaBazar Storefront — Free online store for any local merchant
  • ManaBazar Aggregator — Discover stores by category, location, and offers
  • YouPharma — Complete pharmacy management with GST, batch tracking, and drug license compliance
  • YouRetail — POS billing, inventory, barcode scanning, and GSTR-1 for retail stores
  • YouResto — KOT, table management, and menu builder for restaurants

Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?▼
Browse stores near you, select products, and add them to your cart. At checkout, choose your delivery address and payment method (COD or UPI). You can also place orders via WhatsApp — the order details are pre-filled for you.
Is there a delivery charge?▼
Delivery charges are set by each store individually. Many stores offer free delivery above a minimum order value (shown on their store page). Look for the "Free Delivery" tag when browsing stores.
How do I track my order?▼
After placing an order, you will receive updates via WhatsApp from the store. You can also check order status in the "My Orders" section. Orders go through these stages: Placed → Confirmed → Preparing → Out for Delivery → Delivered.
What payment methods are accepted?▼
Currently we support Cash on Delivery (COD) and UPI direct-to-merchant payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm). Card payments will be available in a future update.
How do I apply a coupon or discount?▼
During checkout, tap "Apply Coupon" to see available offers for that store. You can enter a coupon code manually or browse available discounts. The discount will be applied to your cart total automatically.
Can I cancel or modify my order?▼
You can cancel or modify your order before the store confirms it. Contact the store directly via WhatsApp (available on their store page) for any changes after confirmation.
How do I register my store on ManaBazar?▼
Registration is free! Tap "For Merchants" in the footer, or contact us on WhatsApp. We will help you set up your catalogue and get your store live within 24 hours.
Is ManaBazar available in Telugu?▼
Yes! We support both English and Telugu (తెలుగు). You can switch the language from your Account settings. Our software products (YouPharma, YouRetail, YouResto) also support Telugu.

Still need help?

Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 9 PM.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ManaBazar, we collect information that you provide directly:

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, and delivery addresses when you register or place orders.
  • Order Information: Products ordered, store details, payment method, delivery address, and order history.
  • Location Data: Your approximate location (with your permission) to show nearby stores and calculate delivery distance.
  • Device Information: Device type, browser, and app version for troubleshooting and improving our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show you relevant stores and products near your location
  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order updates and delivery notifications via WhatsApp/SMS
  • To improve our platform and personalise your experience
  • To help merchants understand their customer base (aggregated, not individual data)

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only as follows:

  • With Merchants: Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared with the store you order from, solely for order fulfilment.
  • WhatsApp: When you choose WhatsApp ordering, your message is sent directly to the merchant's WhatsApp number.
  • We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on servers in India. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Payment information is not stored on our servers — UPI payments are processed directly between you and the merchant.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request a copy of your data at any time
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • You can opt out of promotional messages
  • You can revoke location permission from your device settings

6. Contact

2. Platform Role

ManaBazar acts as a technology intermediary connecting customers with local merchants. We are not a seller — all products are sold directly by the respective merchants. We facilitate discovery, ordering, and communication between customers and stores.

3. Orders & Payments

  • Orders placed via ManaBazar are confirmed by the merchant, not by us.

When you use the WhatsApp ordering feature, your order message is sent directly to the merchant's WhatsApp number. The order fulfilment, communication, and any disputes are handled directly between you and the merchant. ManaBazar is not responsible for orders placed via WhatsApp.

6. Customer Responsibilities

7. Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellations before merchant confirmation are free. After confirmation, cancellation policies are set by each merchant. For COD orders, no refund is applicable. For UPI orders, refunds are processed by the merchant directly.
